Helpline:

The Helpline was established in 2009 and was coordinated until December 2014 by the Pancyprian Co-ordination Committee And Welfare of the Child (PSEPEK) within the framework of the European co-funded CyberEthics projects. From January 2015,   The CyberEthics Helpline is coordinated by the Institute of Neuroscience and Technology of Cyprus (CNTI)  Co-funded by the European Connecting Europe Facility. The Helpline gives the public an opportunity to answer  Questions and to express his or her concerns about harmful Internet behaviors, harmful communication and Harmful content on the internet. The Line provides psychological and technical support.

Helpline deals with :

- Electronic Harassment Commercial risks / threats
- Electronic seduction
- Illegal Access / Illegal Intervention
- Identity theft
- Other potentially harmful content
- Internet reputation
- Personal Data Theft
- Privacy Settings
- Sexual Content Messages (Sexting)
- Sexual Harassment
- Spam
- Unwanted contact by strangers
